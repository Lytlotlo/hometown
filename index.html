A few activities to do at Johannesburg

Craddle of Humankind World Heritage Site

Life first emerged about 3.8-billion years ago. Our journey begins in South Africa, where fossils of some of the earliest known life forms on Earth have been found. Get to expirence this world heritage site.

GoldReef City

Come have a thrilling ride at Gold Reef City GoldReef City is an amusement park located on an old gold mine which closed in 1971, the park is themed around the gold rush that started in 1886 on the Witwatersrand.

Pilannesburg game drive

Come have a big chance of expirencing the big 5 in their natural habitat. With the amazing scenery and rich ecological richness, this is the place to be!
